Rules

  • Two players take turns.
  • On your turn, remove exactly one full row or column.
  • You may only remove lines that still contain marbles.
  • The player who removes the last marble loses.
  • Center rule: Removing a line through the center forces you to remove an extra line of the opposite type.
  • You may not remove a line with only one marble unless no other move exists.
